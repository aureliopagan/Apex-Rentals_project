Platform Features

A comprehensive solution connecting luxury asset owners with discerning clients

Dual Portal System

Separate interfaces for asset owners and clients, each with role-specific dashboards. Owners manage their fleet while clients browse and book seamlessly with full authentication and security.

Smart Booking Management

Dynamic availability tracking prevents double-bookings. Assets automatically update their status, and owners receive instant booking requests with detailed client information and date ranges.

Premium Asset Categories

Purpose-built for the luxury market with dedicated sections for yachts, exotic cars, and private jets. Each category features tailored color themes and filtering options for an elevated user experience.

Our Story

We both love luxury vehicles—the engineering, the experience, all of it. When we started looking into the rental market, we noticed something: there's no platform that handles yachts, exotic cars, and private jets all in one place. Especially not in Puerto Rico.

That's where Apex Rentals comes in. Asset owners need a reliable way to rent out their inventory without dealing with unverified clients or booking conflicts. Renters want a trustworthy place to find luxury assets without the usual hassle. We built this as the intermediary—verified sellers, smart booking management that prevents double-bookings, and a straightforward interface.

This is our capstone project for Holberton School, representing everything we've learned about building solutions to actual problems.
